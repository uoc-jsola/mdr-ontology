Ontology Specification Draft

Abstract

This ontology models medical devices, incidents, risks, and corrective actions under European MDR/IVDR regulations. It captures the relationships between devices, manufacturers, risk classifications, and incident management processes, supporting semantic interoperability and structured data representation. The ontology was developed as part of the PAC2 assignment for the knowledge representation course at the UOC.
